# Further Development of a Simple R Package (aLBI) for the Estimation of Stock Status from the Length Frequency Data

### **Academic Profile**
* **Degree:** Master of Science (M.S.) in Fisheries Management
* **Institution:** University of Chittagong, Bangladesh
* **Author:** Ataher Ali
* **Supervisor:** Associate Professor Mohammed Shahidul Alam

---

## **Project Overview**
This repository contains the complete research data, R scripts, and documentation for my M.S. thesis. The research focused on enhancing the **aLBI** R package, which provides a simplified framework for estimating fish stock status using length-based indicators—a crucial tool for data-limited fisheries.

### **Key Achievements**
* Developed and refined the **aLBI** R package, now available on CRAN.
* Applied these models to estimate stock status from length-frequency data.
* The findings from this thesis were published as a first-author research article in the journal *Fisheries Research* (2025).

---

## **Repository Structure**
* `/Data`: Raw and processed length-frequency data used in the analysis. Too many folders, subfolders.
* `/Scripts`: R scripts used for development, model testing, and stock status estimation.
* `/Thesis`: Documentation regarding the thesis submission to the University of Chittagong.
